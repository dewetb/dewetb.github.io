Together Technologies

A mentorship program for young men from under privileged areas around Cape Town. We run a Ruby on Rails Bootcamp & mentor these men in all the skills they would need to enter a fruitful career in web development & take good care of their family

Langana

Langana is a web app that solves 2 different problems for 2 different parts of our society. South Africans with more privilege need help finding trustworthy, hardworking people to hire to do the jobs that can be outsourced, this will free them up to do more revenue producing activities that create even more jobs. Those without privilege needs a way to show that they have a track record of reliable excellent work and that they are hardworking & trustworthy.
